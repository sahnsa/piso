TVA Project

Project Overview

Doumpa is a town in Liberia with a population of 4,000. The Doumpa organization is a Nonprofit organization established by locals to promote community development and unity. Their clinic project aims to provide affordable and modern healthcare services to the people of Doumpa.

My goal

My aim was to develop a user-friendly website that aligned with the organization's vision of enhancing the township. The website supports digital engagement and plans to include a "Clinic Portal" to facilitate doctor's patient record updates, for smooth clinic operations.

Future Plans

The Doumpa organization plans to continuously improve its website to meet future needs and align with their vision. The team is excited to celebrate the successful launch of their site and committed to shaping its ongoing development based on their objectives.
